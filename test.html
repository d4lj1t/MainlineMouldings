It's school photo time...
The school photograph is as firm a fixture in the autumn calendar as Halloween or bonfire night. It’s a priceless part of family history and deserves to be framed with special care and consideration. We have a wide and interesting selection of mouldings for the job, catering for all budgets and both modern and conventional styles of school photography.
Whether it’s for a natural family shot or the more formal school format, have a browse through some of these great value ranges: Orange Box, 302s, 16x14mm Polcore Flats, 25mm Polcore Bevels, and Linear. Our versatile Outline pictured below has thin lines of colour on both edges and is ideal for complementing colours within the photos. It's available in three different widths.
Value Photoframe
Our Polcore Basics® Value Photoframe is pretty self-explanatory! A no-frills budget choice available in three attractive finishes and two different widths – economy moulding at it’s best. From £0.54 per metre.
Linear
Our popular Linear moulding is another practical photo frame range offering a good selection of colours and finishes on 15 and 16mm flat profiles at the keenest possible prices. From our Polcore Basics® collection, there's an extra 20% off when ordering two or more boxes. From £0.45 per metre.
